About the Book

One little mouse.
One big, wide, wonderful world.

Max is a small but mighty mouse who lives in the cozy barn on McDoogles' farm. One sunny morning he packs his swag — a cuddly rug, a family photo, and some peppered snacks — and sets off to seek his fortune in the great wide world.

Along the way he meets Hermione the studious hedgehog, cheerful Camilla the cow, mud-loving Penelope Pig, and his friend Quack the duck. But every friend whispers the same warning… Beware of Knife-fang!

Up and Off
🌸 ✨ 🌸

Once upon a time, there lived a small, fit, young mouse called Max. His full name was Maxamillion, but that was too difficult to spell, so he used the shortened version — Max — which he thought was short and sweet, just like him.

Max had marvellous muscles that others just dreamed of. He worked out in his mouse-made gym every day, as regular as clockwork. His quads were particularly shapely and his bi-cepts were a joy to behold. 💪

One bright morning, Max packed up his cuddly rug, a photo of his family and some peppered, gluten-free snacks in his swag, said his farewells, and headed off to explore the big, wide, wonderful world.

He was in such a hurry he almost didn't hear his mother's final words of dire warning…

"Beware of Knife-fang, the flea-ridden ferocious feline that lives in the McDoogle house!" 🐾

"I will, Mum!" Max called as he scampered off…

About the Author

Wendy B. Watson

Storyteller. Dreamer. Lover of small adventures.

Wendy believes that the best stories are the ones that make children giggle, wonder, and dream of what's just beyond the barn door. With a notebook always in her pocket and an imagination as wide as the open sky, she writes tales filled with mischief, friendship, and just the right pinch of pepper.

Max the Mouse: Up and Off is the first in a series of farmyard adventures — because every great explorer has to start somewhere, even if it's just outside the barn.
