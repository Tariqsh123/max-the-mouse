About the Book

One little mouse.
One big, wide, wonderful world.

Max is a small but mighty mouse who lives in the cozy barn on McDoogles' farm. One sunny morning he packs his swag — a cuddly rug, a family photo, and some peppered snacks — and sets off to seek his fortune in the great wide world.

Along the way he meets Hermione the studious hedgehog, cheerful Camilla the cow, mud-loving Penelope Pig, and his friend Quack the duck. But every friend whispers the same warning… Beware of Knife-fang!
